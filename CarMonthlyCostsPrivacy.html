Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Car Monthly Costs application.

Information Collection and Use

The Car Monthly Costs App does not collect any personal data or usage data.

The Car Monthly Costs App operates entirely locally on your Mac. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or usage data externally. All data created or managed by the app remains exclusively on your device and is never shared or transmitted over the network.

No data processed or created by the Car Monthly Costs App leaves your device. Car Monthly Costs App stores data locally in memory and not on disk.
