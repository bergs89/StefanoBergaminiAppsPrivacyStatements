Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Daily Battery Time App application.

Information Collection and Use

The Daily Battery Time App does not collect any personal data or usage data.

The Daily Battery Time App operates entirely locally on your Mac. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or usage data externally. All data created or managed by the app remains exclusively on your device and is never shared or transmitted over the network.

No data processed or created by the Daily Battery Time App leaves your device. Daily Battery Time App stores data locally in memory and not on disk.
