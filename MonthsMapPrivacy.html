Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the MonthsMap application.

Information Collection and Use

MonthsMap does not collect any personal data or usage data.

MonthsMap operates entirely locally on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or usage data externally. All data created or managed by the app remains exclusively on your device and is never shared or transmitted over the network.

No data processed or created by MonthsMap leaves your device. MonthsMap stores data locally using UserDefaults.
