Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Heat Pump Savings App application.

Information Collection and Use

The Heat Pump Savings App collects only the data you provide to use its features.

User-Provided Data

Heat Pump Savings App allows you to create and manage energy calculations by entering heating costs, efficiency ratings, usage patterns, and other calculation parameters. This data is provided voluntarily and stored only in connection with your use of the app.

iCloud Storage

To enhance your experience by syncing your data across your devices, we use Apple's iCloud service. All energy calculations, saved scenarios, and associated metadata are stored in your iCloud account. No personal data beyond what you enter into the app is collected.

Data Usage

The information you provide is used solely to operate and improve Heat Pump Savings App. We use your data to:

  • Store and manage your energy calculations.
  • Synchronize your data across your devices using iCloud.
  • Enable features such as saving and retrieving calculation scenarios.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage and Security

iCloud Integration: Your data is securely stored in your iCloud account, managed under Apple's robust security and privacy guidelines. We rely on Apple's infrastructure to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Local Security Measures: We implement standard security measures to protect your data within the app. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your iCloud account.
