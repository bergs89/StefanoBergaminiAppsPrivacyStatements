Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your data when you use our application on macOS. By using Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection

User-Provided Data

Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App allows you to create and manage solar energy calculations by entering data such as system size, location, energy usage, and any associated details. This data is provided voluntarily and stored only in connection with your use of the app.

iCloud Storage

To enhance your experience by syncing your data across your devices, we use Apple's iCloud service. All solar calculation entries and associated metadata are stored in your iCloud account. No personal data beyond what you enter into the app is collected.

2. Data Usage

The information you provide is used solely to operate and improve Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App. We use your data to:

  • Store and manage your solar energy calculations.
  • Synchronize your data across your devices using iCloud.
  • Enable features such as saving, recalling, and managing your solar scenarios.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

iCloud Integration: Your data is securely stored in your iCloud account, managed under Apple's robust security and privacy guidelines. We rely on Apple's infrastructure to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Local Security Measures: We implement standard security measures to protect your data within the app. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your iCloud account.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App does not share your data with third-party services except as required to use iCloud for data synchronization. Any third-party services used are subject to their own privacy policies.

We may disclose your information if required by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation.
  • Protect and defend our rights or property.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the app.

5. User Rights

Access and Correction: You have the right to access, modify, or delete the personal data you provide. You can manage your data directly within the app or through your iCloud account.

Consent Withdrawal: You may withdraw your consent to our data practices by discontinuing the use of Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App. Please note that this may affect the functionality of the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Solar Energy Calculator Mobile App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
